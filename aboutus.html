About Us

MealBridge Where Leftovers Find Purpose.

MealBridge is a platform that connects individuals, restaurants, and food vendors with surplus food to nearby NGOs, shelters, and community kitchens that need it most.

Every day, tons of perfectly edible food go to waste, while millions go hungry. We built MealBridge to close that gap — to bridge the surplus to the need.

What We Do

  • Make it easy for food donors to log and list leftover meals.
  • Notify nearby organizations in real time.
  • Provide a simple, clean dashboard for both donors and receivers.
  • Promote sustainability, empathy, and community support.

Why We Started

We observed how much food is discarded daily in our communities — often due to lack of awareness or access to donation channels. Instead of just noticing the problem, we decided to do something about it.

As two developers with a heart for social change, we believe technology can be a powerful tool for good. MealBridge is our contribution — a small step toward a more mindful and nourished world.

Join Us

Whether you're a restaurant owner, a student with extra food from an event, or an NGO looking to help — you're part of the bridge. Let's work together to reduce waste and share meals with love.
